Interstellar

15 may 19:00 .

Near future, the population of the Earth is on the verge of extinction. Hoping to find a new habitable planet, a group of explorers undertakes the most important mission in the history of the humankind — they travel beyond the bounds of our galaxy through a space-time tunnel — a ‘black hole.’ The journey has one aim — to learn, whether the humankind has a chance for salvation and future.
